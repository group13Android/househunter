What does House Hunter do?

House Hunter acts as a simple and easy to use platform between landlords and people looking to rent accomadation. It provides the oppurtunity to find the perfect accomodation for your needs. Landlords may list their houses they wish to make avaialble. They may then connect via our House Hunter Chat. 

What do I need to do to get Started?

Simply download our app and create an account. It's as simple as that. There are no other actions required. You can login with the credentials goetz@demo.com and password 123456.
Enter some details about yourself and you can begin your search for your perfect house!  

What do I need to get the app running on my device?

The app should run with no difficulties from API Level 21 and above. Also if you are encountering issues on an emulator you may need to update your Google Play Services Verison from the Google Play Store

Running the Project from Source Code

If you are running the Source code all you have to do is clone the latest code from our Github Repo. No other Setup required! All of our pointer to firebase databases and Google Maps etc are in our internal code and therfore no pointing to external sources is needed
